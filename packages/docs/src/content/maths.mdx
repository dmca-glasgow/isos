Equations are numbered, and when labelled (with prefix `eq`) can be referenced like so:

<CodeSnippet latex={String.raw`

\documentclass{article}
\usepackage{amsmath}
\usepackage[noabbrev, capitalise, nameinlink]{cleveref}
\begin{document}

\begin{equation}
\label{eq:myref1}
x^2 - 5 x + 6 = 0
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
x^2 - 5 x + 6 = 0
\end{equation}

Check out \autoref{eq:myref1} and \autoref{eq:myref2}.

\end{document}
